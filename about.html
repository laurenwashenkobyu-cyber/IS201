About Me

Hello! I'm Lauren Willis, a dedicated student pursuing my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Accounting at Brigham Young University.

I'm passionate about helping people achieve financial success. Currently, I work as a Money Max Account Coach at United Financial Freedom, where I guide clients through personalized financial strategies to optimize their money management.

When I'm not studying or working, you can find me at the gym—fitness is a huge part of my life and helps me stay focused and energized. I believe in the power of discipline, whether it's in finances, academics, or physical health.

I'm actively involved in the Accounting Society and Women In Accounting at BYU, where I connect with fellow students who share my passion for the field. My goal is to become a CPA and help individuals and businesses achieve their financial objectives.
